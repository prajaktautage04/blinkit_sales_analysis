# blinkit_sales_analysis
This project focuses on analyzing Blinkit’s end-to-end business performance using real-world–style data. The objective is to derive actionable business insights across sales, customers, products, delivery operations, and marketing performance. The analysis follows a complete data analytics workflow. 

#Buisness insights:-
Analyze overall sales, profit, and order trends (2023–2024)
Understand customer behavior and lifetime value across segments
Evaluate product and category performance
Measure delivery efficiency and customer sentiment
Assess marketing campaign effectiveness and ROI
Enable data-driven decision-making for business stakeholders

#Tools and Technologies:-
Python: Data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis (Pandas, NumPy)
SQL: Data querying, joins, aggregations, and KPI calculations
Power BI: Interactive dashboards, DAX measures, and business visualizations
